Echoes of Silence

Echoes of Silence is the third mixtape by Canadian singer  The Weeknd, released December 21, 2011 via his website The release follows his Polaris Music Prize nominated debut release House of Balloons and his second mixtape  Thursday, both released earlier the same year. The project is the final installment in the trilogy of free albums released by The Weeknd in 2011

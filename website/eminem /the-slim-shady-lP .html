The Slim Shady LP

The Slim Shady LP is the second studio album by American rapper Eminem. It was released on February 23, 1999, under Interscope Records and Dr. Dre's Aftermath Entertainment. Recorded inFerndale, Michigan following Eminem's recruitment by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine,the album features production from Dr. Dre, the Bass Brothers and Eminem himself. The majority of the record's lyrical content is written from the perspective of the rapper's  alter ego Slim Shady, whom the rapper created on Slim Shady Ep (1997). The lyrics are noted for their over-the-top depictions of violence and heavy use of profanity.

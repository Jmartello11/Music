Section.80

Section.80 is the debut Studio almbum by Kendrick Lamar. It was released on July 2, 2011, by Top Dawg Entertainment. The album features guest apperances from GLC, Colin Munroe, Ashtrobot, Bj the Chicago Kid, School Boy Q, Ab-Soul and vocals from late singer-songwriter Alori Joh. The production  was mainly handled by Top Dawg in-house producers\ from production group Digi+Phonics, along with THC, YTommy Black, Wyldfyer, Terrace Martin, and J.Cole.
